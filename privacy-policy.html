🔒 Privacy Policy

📅 Last Updated: December 15, 2024 | Effective Date: December 15, 2024

Welcome to PlayCrazyGames (https://raja1068.github.io/playcrazygames). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our games.

📌 Quick Summary: We do NOT collect personal information. We use cookies only for ads and analytics. All game data (high scores) is stored locally on your device. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We respect your privacy and collect minimal information:

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you visit PlayCrazyGames, our servers may automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, game interactions
  • IP Address: Anonymized for analytics purposes

1.2 Local Storage Information

We use your browser's local storage to save:

  • Game High Scores: Your best scores in Snake Game
  • Game Progress: Level progress in Gun Shooting game
  • Preferences: Dark mode preference (if enabled)

Note: All this data stays on YOUR device. We NEVER access or store it on our servers.

1.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • ❌ Names, emails, or phone numbers
  • ❌ Payment information (we have no payments)
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Contacts or social media data
  • ❌ Biometric data
  • ❌ Sensitive personal information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the limited information we collect to:

  • ✅ Provide, operate, and maintain our games
  • ✅ Improve user experience and game performance
  • ✅ Analyze usage patterns to optimize our website
  • ✅ Serve relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • ✅ Protect against fraud and security threats

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience:

3.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site
  • Advertising Cookies: Used by Google AdSense to show relevant ads
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings (dark mode, etc.)

3.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Site Permissions → Cookies

Note: Disabling cookies may affect game functionality.

We use trusted third-party services to enhance our website:

4.1 Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to our site and other sites. Learn more:

  • Google AdSense Privacy Policy

4.2 Google Analytics (Optional)

4.3 GitHub Pages

Our website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server logs. View their privacy policy:

  • GitHub Privacy Statement

PlayCrazyGames is family-friendly and suitable for all ages. However:

  • Parents are encouraged to supervise children's online activities

In compliance with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act), we do not require any personal information to play our games.
